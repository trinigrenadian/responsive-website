wedding package

Laluna’s small, intimate setting in a secluded bay puts romance at center stage

Laluna’s quiet beach is the ideal location for your Grenada destination wedding. Our boutique resort’s Caribbean setting is what you’ve always dreamed of for your beach ceremony: Tropical flowers and lush greenery wrap the white sand beach, and the crystal clear sea, brilliant with colors of blue and green, quietly laps the shore. Say your vows under a Caribbean wedding arch while your invited guests watch on and share your special day. Following the ceremony, stay on-resort, as the Laluna Sunset Lounge and Italian Restaurant can be transformed to an inviting reception venue. Laluna’s dedicated staff have been planning destination weddings for over 14 years. From arranging the photographer to ordering the cake to helping with the marriage license, our staff helps you with it all. Give as much or as little input as you like, and we will take care of the details. To take the guesswork out of Grenada destination wedding planning, we have special packages that include everything needed to create your perfect wedding in paradise. Laluna's Seven Night Wedding Program for Two Includes:

Accommodation for the bride and groom in a romantic Cottage Suite with private veranda with plunge pool An intimate and relaxing Balinese couples massage Daily yoga/meditation sessions A pampering manicure and pedicure for both the bride and groom Delicious meals in Laluna's Italian restaurant with daily a-la-carte American breakfast and dinner (excluding beverages) A bottle of Prosecco A beautiful wedding cake A locally crafted bridal bouquet and groom's boutonniere A professional wedding photographer, which includes digital files of all photos.
